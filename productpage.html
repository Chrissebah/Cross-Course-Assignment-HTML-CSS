Game Overview


Forge legend is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game that was released in the summer of 2022.
Set in a universe where elves are at war with a misterious enemy of the dark, you must fight your way through many dungeons, bosses and build up your power through companions, items and friends.
You are able to create your own character and choose what type of fighter you are, and how you will help your side win the battles and war. A few skill classes you can choose from are: brute, healer, sourcerer, tank and many more.
This game was given a 98% rating at launch and is still mainting a rating of 94% currently.
